The Scrip program not only creates savings for you, but also supports the High School Choir programs

Top Benefits of Scrip Fundraising

  • No asking families to sell unwanted or unnecessary products to family and firends
  • It's easy for a single shcool family to generate $600 - $1,000 a year or more
  • Everyday family purchases become consistent earnings for your organization
  • Scrip earnings (3%-15%) are greater than credit card rewards

How Scrip Fundraising works

  1. When families order scrip they pay full face value for gift cards
  2. If someone orders $100 gift card for the local grocery store the Scrip Club receives a 2%-13% discount depending on retailer
  3. The % discount is then awarded to the Scrib Club as a rebate
